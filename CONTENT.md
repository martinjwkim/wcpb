## Programs

### Group Lessons (/lessons — "Your Game, Your Way.")
Intro (verbatim): "No matter where you are on your pickleball journey, we've
got the perfect lesson to help you step up your game. Our professional
instructors are dedicated to breaking down the game and building up your
skills, all while keeping it fun, competitive, and packed with valuable
insights."

- **Beginner** — "Perfect for anyone just getting started." Good for: anyone
  who has already taken the Introductory Clinic, or beginners with limited
  knowledge of the basics.
  1. Paddle grip, positioning, serve and return basics
  2. Return depth, volleying, and "nice nice mean" drill
  3. Game rules, volley review, simple gameplay
  4. Gameplay strategy and positioning for success
- **Intermediate** — "Ready to elevate your game?" Good for: advanced
  beginners or low intermediate, skill level 2.75 to 3.25.
  1. Improving shot consistency, making your opponent move
  2. Advanced dinking, drop shots, and drive shot drills
  3. Smash defense, lob strategies
  4. Gameplay tactics, court awareness, poaching techniques
- **Advanced** — "Fine-tune your skills and master the court." Good for:
  intermediate players in the 3.5 to 3.75 skill range looking to improve to
  a 4.0+ player.
  1. Enhancing playbook, advanced shot techniques
  2. Strategic gameplay, offensive/defensive balance
  3. Smashing and advanced lobbing tactics
  4. Mastering court positioning and advanced team play

### Gameplay guide (/gameplay — educational content, verbatim source for any rules copy)
- Basics: paddle + perforated plastic ball, badminton-sized court, games to
  11 win-by-2; underhand diagonal serve; double-bounce rule; the kitchen =
  7-ft non-volley zone.
- Strategy: dinking, third-shot drop, volleys & smashes timing, court
  awareness.
- Doubles (teamwork, communication, positioning) vs singles (movement,
  placement, footwork).
